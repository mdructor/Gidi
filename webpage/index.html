Gidi

A new way to play.

Turn all of those hard-earned hours of muscle memory you have built up on your gamepad into a new way to create.

Gidi maps your controller input into MIDI output, transforming your controller into a powerful digital instrument. Use Gidi with any DAW(Ableton, FLStudio, Logic, etc) or any MIDI synth and be on your way to creating beautiful melodies and harmony without ever having to touch a keyboard.

Plug in -> play

Gidi detects and connects to any type of gamepad you have (PS4, Xbox, Steam, etc). It will also detect available MIDI ports to send output to, allowing you to connect to any digital synth imaginable and a lifetime of creative endeavors.

Infinite possibilities

Gidi gives your controller the full range of the keyboard and comes with pre-defined control mappings to help you get started with your new instrument. Mappings are fully extendable and new intuitive control schemes for different styles of music are always on the horizon.

Get it while its fresh!

Gidi is still young and is growing. You can get access to the available beta builds on the download page. We love to get your feature requests and bug reports. Gidi is also fully open-source and free forever! Check us out on Github.
